About the Tournament

Melbourne’s Premier
T10 Cricket Event

Darebin Chargers is proud to present the T10 Championship 2026 — a fast-paced senior cricket tournament bringing competitive teams together for an exciting short-format competition across Melbourne’s northern suburbs.

The tournament runs from 22 August to 6 September 2026, with games on Saturdays and Sundays at grounds in Epping, Lalor and Thomastown.

With 24 teams competing through 4 league games, Quarter Finals, Semi Finals and a Grand Final, every ball counts. Matches are played with Kookaburra white balls under qualified umpires, with Man of the Match medals awarded every game.

Team entries are confirmed on a first-come, reviewed basis. Spots are strictly limited — register early to secure your place.

Tournament Format

How It Works

24 teams across four knockout stages. 10 overs per side — fast, electric cricket from ball one.

1
League Stage
All 24 teams play 4 league games. Points determine Quarter Final seeding.
›
2
Quarter Finals
Top teams advance to Quarter Finals. Single-elimination knockout matches.
›
3
Semi Finals
The final four compete. Two spots in the Grand Final on the line.
›
4
Grand Final
The championship decider. One team lifts the trophy and $10,000.
